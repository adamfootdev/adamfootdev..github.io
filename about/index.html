About

About Me

Hi, I'm Adam, an indie developer building apps for iOS and other Apple platforms using the Swift programming language! I started learning to build apps using Swift in 2015 as a hobby alongside school work. Since then I have worked on many hobby projects that I have released on the App Store. After finishing studying, I worked at a startup for nearly four years. In 2022, I quit my job and became a full-time indie developer. I now spend most of my days building these previous hobby projects alongside a small amount of contract work!

If you'd like to get in touch, please feel free to Contact Me.

Experience

Indie Work

iOS Developer, January 2016 - Present

I started creating apps as a hobby after teaching myself iOS development using Swift. I have had experience developing for the majority of Apple’s platforms using UIKit, AppKit and, have more recently switched to using SwiftUI. The apps I build are quick to adopt new Apple APIs, such as Widgets and Siri Shortcuts.

After working in a start-up environment full-time since 2018, I decided to quit my job and become an indie developer full-time in 2022.
