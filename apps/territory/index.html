Territory

Storefront Switcher for the App Store

About App

Territory is a tool for iOS and other Apple platform developers to quickly switch the active storefront of the App Store on their device. This is so that they can see if their app has been featured in other country/region.

Featured On

Territory has been featured on 9to5Mac and iOS Dev Tools.

Platforms

Territory is available on iOS, iPadOS, macOS, tvOS, visionOS and watchOS here.

Technologies

Territory has been built using SwiftUI, App Intents, StoreKit and WidgetKit.
