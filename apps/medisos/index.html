MediSOS

Medical Emergency Alarm

About App

MediSOS allows you to activate a medical emergency siren when needed most. MediSOS syncs with MediWidget and MediWear for a full medical ID experience.

Platforms

MediSOS is available on iOS, iPadOS and watchOS here.

Technologies

MediSOS has been built using SwiftUI, AVKit, Keychain and WidgetKit.
