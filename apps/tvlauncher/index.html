TV Launcher

Quickly Launch Live TV channels

About App

TV Launcher provides users with a convenient way to quickly launch live TV channels within their respective apps across Apple platforms. Made for cord-cutters in the UK who were primarily using their Apple TV for media consumption, and now also available in the US, Australia and Ireland, TV Launcher has a convenient TV Guide for being able to find something live to watch with ease.

Featured On

TV Launcher has been featured on iMore (1), iMore (2) and 9to5Mac.

Platforms

TV Launcher is available on iOS, iPadOS, macOS, tvOS, visionOS and watchOS here.

Technologies

TV Launcher has been built using SwiftUI, UIKit, App Intents, Core Data, Core Data + CloudKit, TipKit, UserNotifications and WidgetKit.
